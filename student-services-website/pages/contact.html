FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common student questions.

How do I reset my student portal password?

Visit the login page and click "Forgot Password". A reset link will be sent to your registered email within minutes.

When does course registration open?

Registration opens 4 weeks before each semester. You'll receive an email notification with your specific registration window.

How do I apply for a scholarship?

Log into the student portal, navigate to Financial Aid, and complete the scholarship application form before the deadline.

Is counseling services free for students?

Yes! All enrolled students have free access to up to 6 counseling sessions per semester. Book via the portal or call us directly.
